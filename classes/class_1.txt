Class: Pot

Attributes:
on_burner (boolean)
lid_is_on (boolean)
pot_is_boiling (boolean)
time_on_burner (integer)
total_capacity (integer)
current_capacity (integer)

Methods:
calculate_percent_full (divides current_capacity by total_capacity)
fill (adds 100 to current_capacity)
put_on_burner (toggles on_burner to true)
boil_pot (if lid_is_on=true and time_on_burner > 5 toggles pot_is_boiling to true)
